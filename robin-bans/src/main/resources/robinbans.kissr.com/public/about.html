Robin is an illustrator with over 20 years professional experience.

Being a native of Glasgow, Robin has always drawn inspiration from the people and surroundings of his home town. Indeed many aspects of his artwork are often a reflection of the everyday humour and character you find in the city.

His speciality over the years has been children's book illustration, children's TV and animation storyboarding. In addition he has a wide variety of published works which span from graphic design to landscape art.

One of Robin's great pleasures is in working closely with individuals on personal commissions and projects. Such projects have included wedding artwork compilations, cartoons, music band album cover design etc

Over the years Robin has experimented with many different types of media which include watercolor, acrylics, pastels, spray paint, computer art, anything in fact that will help achieve the desired effect.

So without further adieu, have a browse through the galleries and enjoy some of Robin's work.
